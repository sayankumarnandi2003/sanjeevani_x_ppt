India faces a critical dual challenge: managing a massive population of 1.4 billion while battling a shortage of medical infrastructure. This presentation details our proposal for Sanjeevani, a platform that leverages:

Diagnostic AI: Automated radiology (ChestX-ray14) and Onco-pathology.

Operational AI: Predictive resource management for hospital beds and oxygen.

Frugal Innovation: Designing for low-cost, high-access scenarios suitable for rural India.
